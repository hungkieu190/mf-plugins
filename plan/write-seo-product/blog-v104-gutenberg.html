Your Course Insights, Now Portable: The "Export PDF" Revolution

The LearnPress Sticky Notes Update version 1.0.4 brings the most requested feature: PDF Export. While digital notes are convenient, many learners still prefer the tangibility of paper or the permanence of a PDF document. Consequently, we have made it possible to take your notes anywhere.

[Image: Screenshot showing the new Export PDF button in the View All Notes popup. Alt Text: LearnPress Sticky Notes Update v1.0.4 PDF Export Feature]

By clicking the "Export PDF" button, students can generate a document containing all their notes for a specific course. The file is automatically named mynotes-{username}-{domain}.pdf. This ensures that every export is unique and easy to identify. Furthermore, the layout is clean and professional, making it perfect for physical printing or digital archiving.

Why is this important for LearnPress Student Productivity? Studies show that reviewing notes in different formats can enhance memory retention. By offering a PDF option, you are giving your students a powerful tool for success. Additionally, it adds a premium feel to your LMS platform.

Never Miss a Detail with "View Full"

Some notes are short reminders, while others are long observations. Previously, reading long notes in the sticky view could feel cramped. In this LearnPress Sticky Notes Update, we have introduced the "View Full" button. This toggle is available within the `[lp_sticky_notes]` shortcode.

When a student clicks "View Full," the note expands to reveal the entire content. Once they are finished reading, they can easily collapse it back. This ensures that the student dashboard remains clean and organized. It is all about balance—providing enough space for deep thoughts without cluttering the user interface.

  • Intelligent Toggling: Switch between summary and full view instantly.
  • Improved Readability: No more scrolling inside tiny boxes.
  • UI Cleanliness: Keep the dashboard focused on what matters.

A Polished Experience on Every Device

We know that many students learn on the go. However, mobile screens are small and precious. In previous versions, the sticky button sometimes overlapped critical navigation elements, such as the LearnPress "Next" button. We fixed this in the latest LearnPress Sticky Notes Update.

We have added a new "Button Bottom Offset (px)" setting in the Appearance menu. This allows site owners to precisely position the sticky button. Whether you need it 20px or 100px from the bottom, you now have the control. This ensures a LearnPress Mobile UX Fix that works for every theme.

Additionally, we perfected the "Small" button size. Previously, it could appear slightly oval on certain browsers. Now, it is a perfectly sharp circle. While it might seem like a small detail, aesthetic perfection leads to higher user trust and engagement. A professional site needs professional tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update to version 1.0.4?

Simply go to your WordPress Dashboard, navigate to Plugins, and click "Update Now" next to LP Sticky Notes. Ensure you have an active license.

Where can I find the PDF Export button?

Open the "View All Notes" popup from any lesson. You will see the "Export PDF" button at the top or bottom of the list.

Does the button offset work on all themes?

Yes. The offset setting is applied globally to the sticky button, ensuring compatibility with any WordPress theme or mobile layout.
